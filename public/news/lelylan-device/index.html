Why Lelylan Device?
From a recent study, being able to access or control objects remotely is the most desired functionality for smart products with a 53 percent of consumers expressing interest.

Many companies working in the Internet of Thing (e.g. Bluemix, Xively, Spark Core) offer APIs and basic dashboards to easily control the physical world. This is great butÂ they leave us with one problem: building an extraordinary User Experience for the Internet of Things is difficult.
